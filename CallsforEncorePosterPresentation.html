ADMA2024 Call for Encore Talks

The 20th International Conference on Advanced Data Mining and Applications (ADMA2024) will be held in Sydney, Australia during 3rd ~ 5th December 2024. ADMA2024 aims at bringing together the experts on data mining from around the world and providing a leading international forum for the dissemination of original research findings in data mining, spanning applications, algorithms, software, and systems, as well as different applied disciplines with potential in data mining.

It is our great pleasure to invite you to participate in this premier annual event through delivering Encore talks about your fresh scientific findings and applications of high significance and widespread impact in the areas relevant to data mining. The ADMA Encore track aims to spotlight notable research efforts in the field and encourage inspiring exchanges among academic and professionals. It offers a platform and an exclusive opportunity for researchers and practitioners to share their research work that has already been published, accepted, or is currently in press by esteemed conferences (including but not limited to KDD, ICDM, WSDM, CIKM, ICDE, VLDB, SIGMOD, SIGIR, AAAI, IJCAI, NeurIPS, CVPR, ICCV, etc.) and journals (including but not limited to IEEE TKDE, ACM TKDD, VLDB Journal, ACM TODS, IEEE TPAMI, etc.) relevant to data mining and applications. The Encore talk topics should be relevant to the ADMA2024 Themes and Topics as specified in Call for Papers. The talk information should be submitted to ADMA2024 for review before the deadline. The speaker of an accepted Encore talk must register in full and attend the conference to deliver the talk during a lightning talk session or a poster session, otherwise the talk will not be included in the conference program.

Encore Talk Submission

The Encore Talk submission portal: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ADMA2024.

When creating an Encore Talk submission, the proposer should select the "Proposal & Abstract Track" first. After populating the submission form with information like title, abstract, and authors, the proposer should tick the "Encore Talk Abstract" option under "SUBJECT AREAS". Following this step correctly is important to ensure that the proposal goes to the Encore Track Chairs for review.

Encore Talk Submission Guidelines

ADMA Encore Talk submission should be in PDF format and addresses the following information in an organised and informative way. The review will be based on the compliance to these requirements and the quality and potential impact of the talk.

  • Title: title of the talk.

  • Abstract: up to 200 words, to allow conference attendees to understand the topic and the focus of the talk.

  • Speaker Short bio

  • If the paper is already published, please provided the publication information including publication venue, dates, doi, etc. If the paper is just Accepted / In press, please upload the notification of acceptance in PDF as an attachment. In either case, the paper full text in PDF should be submitted as an attachment. Attachments are submitted into the same upload portal.

  • Suitability: Briefly justify the relevance to ADMA themes and topics, the quality of the publication venue, and why the talk can attract great interest or show significance of the data mining and applications community.

  • Expected target audience and prerequisites.

Enquiries

All enquiries about Encore talks should be emailed to the Encore Track Chairs via adma2024@mq.edu.au, with a subject starting with "[ADMA2024 Encore]".
